How to Fix your Credit

The plan was established and our Credit Help Network experts are now applying.

Both our legal department and our financial department are in contact with the credit bureaus, sending specific requests and making sure that the changes are made, in the optimal speed.

Since our Credit Help Network experts have combined 25 years experience, they will not let anything go and present the desired results. Leave it to them as they know the approach, the rules and fine print, and of course - will know exactly how to use it!

With our well established strategy, our legal experts will request the Credit Bureaus to investigate the creditors and verify the negative information they are reporting according to the guideline s in the FCRA.

If you are worried that it won't work because you think the negative information belongs to you, our Credit Help Network experts might surprise you.

The FCRA states that even if negative information belongs to you, if the information contains any inaccuracies, is not being reported and handled according to the guidelines set by the FCRA or cannot be verified in its entirety, then by law it must be removed. Statistics show that inaccuracies occurs more than 75% of the time in a credit file.
