Credit Repair Reviews

When we work together, our goal is to lead you towards a new beginning, full of opportunities and Free of negative credit.

In order to achieve the goal in the quickest and most effective method, our “Credit Help Network” experts will comprehensively examine all the parameters.

"Credit Help Network" will collect all information that appears in the credit reports and credit scores. Once we have all this information, we divide the items in order of importance.

Your credit report contains your personal information (name address, date of birth, SS#, etc.); it is made up of your entire credit history, both positive and negative, for the last 7 to 10 years. There are many types of negative accounts, like late Payments, Charge off Accounts, Collection Accounts, Public Records (Bankruptcies, Judgments, and Tax Liens).

Since any detail can make a difference, we will present the organized information to you, and together go over every element, making sure we have all the information we need about each item.

We will then be able to spot and identify our specific tools and weapons in the upcoming process. Once our experts know which items they aim to change, they will prepare a tailored plan.

Usually when you turn to a credit repair company, you expect quick results. When our clients ask "how long is it going to take to see results?" we explain that it all depends on your situation and how much work you need done, but most Credit Help Network clients begin to see some results within the first 30-45 days-That's why working with Credit Help Network is the best choice for your credit repair needs.

While we at Credit Help Network work to remove inaccurate negative items from your credit report, we understand that some negative remarks are substantiated. There is still room for improvement! Negative, inaccurate, and erroneous remarks have more to them than meets the eye. Credit card companies will often time share critical information about your financial life. This may include information about your car payments, mortgage, employment and family obligated payments. In these instances Credit Help Network goes to work and evaluates the validity of this information. It is our job to assess the information and the items on the credit report. Our second step will open a new world and you will see what the process can do for you. It might surprise you how much is possible.

Thanks to the collaboration of our finance department and our legal department, we can design and implement a strategy that will serve your needs and lead us towards your ultimate goal, step by step.
